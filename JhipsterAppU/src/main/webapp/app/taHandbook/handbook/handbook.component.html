On-Boarding Information

Congratulations! You have been accepted as a teaching assistant for VCU Computer Science. This position will persist through your time at VCU, but you can set yourself to an active or inactive state each semester along with setting your number of hours available weekly and available times. Please read the following directions to get started. You should have already received an email from MailGun support requesting that the site can send you emails. Follow the directions in the email to accept this request. Go to [url] and register using your VCU email. You will receive a confirmation email, allowing you to complete your registration. Because you are already listed as a TA, you should see the TA view when signed in. From this view, go to the “TA Handbook” tab from the navigation bar and read through all of the instructions. Familiarize yourself with the tutorials in the “Tutorials” tab. You may not need all of this information depending on your role, but keep these resources in mind as an available resource. Next, go to the “Update Information” tab to enter all of the needed information for the current semester. TA Expectations

Information updates Active TAs will receive an email 3 weeks before the beginning of each semester. This email will direct you to update your information on the TA web portal so that course matching is based on the correct information. Including your availabilities is especially important to finding a class that matches your schedule. Deactivation If you are unable to act as a TA for a given semester, please set your status to inactive. Note that this will disable all email notifications, so you must log in and reactivate your account if you wish to continue as a TA in later semesters. Keep in mind that your status will be automatically set to inactive after your graduation date, although this can be reactivated if needed. Timeliness
